# Business Insights 360 ‚Äì AtliQ Hardware

## Overview

AtliQ Hardware, a fast-growing global company, aimed to transition to data-driven decision-making using Power BI for the first time. This end-to-end project, inspired by the Codebasics Power BI course, spans key departments like finance, sales, marketing, supply chain, and executive operations.

- Bookmark navigation & page switching
- KPI indicators, dynamic titles, conditional formatting

## üè¢ Business Context

AtliQ Hardware suffered losses due to intuition-led decisions (e.g., U.S. store launch). This dashboard is part of a strategy shift toward data-backed insights. Data spans retailers, distributors, and direct sales across 27 markets and 75 customers.

# AtliQ Hardware ‚Äì Business Insights 360 Dashboard Summary

This summary covers all five main dashboard pages-Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive-designed to give AtliQ Hardware‚Äôs stakeholders actionable, data-driven insights across the business.

---

## 1. Finance Page
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2aeba1e9-f6d7-41d5-bcc4-58a2e9c1e47e)


- **Key Metrics:**  
  - Net Sales: $3.74B  
  - Gross Margin: 38.1%  
  - Net Profit: -14.0%  
- **Market Performance:**  
  - Largest sales: India, USA, Canada  
  - Most markets have negative net profit %; only a few (e.g., Norway, Pakistan) are positive.
- **Visuals:**  
  - Scatter plot: Net profit % vs. net sales by market  
  - Market-wise breakdown: Net sales, gross margin, net profit %, and YoY change  
  - Net sales & gross margin bifurcation: Gross margin ($1.42B), COGS ($2.31B, 61.9% of sales)
- **Takeaway:**  
  - Strong sales, but profitability remains a challenge in most markets.

## 2. Sales Page
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6caf9ddd-b4e7-4b9e-8d52-5b4fd7ba2ac7)


- **Customer Performance:**  
  - Top customers: Amazon, Atliq Exclusive, Atliq e Store  
  - Gross margin % varies by customer; Atliq Exclusive leads with 46.0%
- **Gross Margin Distribution:**  
  - Overall GM: 38.09%  
  - COGS: 61.92% of sales  
  - Pre-invoice deduction: $1.73B (23.43%)  
  - Post-invoice deduction: $1.91B (25.87%)
- **Visuals:**  
  - Scatter plot: Net sales vs. gross margin % by customer  
  - Bar charts: Top customers by sales and margin
- **Takeaway:**  
  - Major customers drive most revenue and margin; customer profitability is mixed.

## 3. Marketing Page

## **Overall Insights**

- **Sales and market share are strong and growing, especially in India and APAC.**
- **Profitability is a challenge:** Net profit is negative in most regions and segments.
- **Customer concentration:** A few key customers drive most revenue and margin.
- **Supply chain forecasting is improving, but inventory risks remain.**
- **Dashboards enable granular, data-driven decisions across all business functions.**
